Karl Marx: *Das Kapital. Kritik der politischen Ökonomie. Erster Band.*  
Buch I: Der Produktionsprozeß des Kapitals.  
Dietz Verlag Berlin, 1962.

## Required distinction in learning materials

All learning materials must distinguish between:

1. Marx’ original text,
2. didactic explanation,
3. present-day contextualisation,
4. simplified examples used only for learning.

No didactic simplification may be presented as if it were a direct statement from Marx.
